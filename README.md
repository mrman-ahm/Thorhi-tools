# THROHI Website Project

This repository is the source of truth for the complete redesign and production of **throhi.com**.

The website is being developed as a premium B2B product catalogue and inquiry platform for THROHI Medical Tools. Strategy, research, brand decisions, interface specifications, product data, implementation, testing, security, and deployment records belong here.

## Current stage

The strategic foundation, responsive wireframes, design system, and audited high-fidelity homepage are complete. Frontend production is now active: the first Next.js implementation milestone includes the responsive homepage, semantic design tokens, temporary logo and instrument placeholders, accessibility foundations, tests, and CI quality gates.

## Core project rules

- The legacy website is a catalogue source, not a design or copywriting reference.
- No manufacturing, certification, material, export, quality, or OEM claim may be published without evidence and approval.
- The four product divisions are Surgical, Dental, Veterinary, and Beauty.
- Product-code search and the multi-product inquiry builder are core requirements.
- The visual direction combines **Steel After Dark**, **Surgical Light**, and **Precision Archive**.
- Heavy effects are limited to one major hero moment and one secondary signature interaction.
- Catalogue, product, form, and inquiry pages must remain fast, accessible, and practical.
- Final production targets WCAG 2.2 AA and complete responsive behavior.
