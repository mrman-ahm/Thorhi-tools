# THROHI V2 Visual Identity

Status: Sector 2 review candidate

Sector 1 static composition was approved on 2026-07-22. This document governs the color, typography, and material identity layered onto that approved structure.

## Creative principle

THROHI V2 presents precision instruments as engineered objects revealed through light, scale, technical indexing, and controlled color.

The website must remain a usable B2B catalogue. Expressive presentation never replaces search, product routes, inquiry access, verified evidence, or accessible interaction.

## Typography

### Archivo Variable

Role:
- hero declarations
- division names
- major section headings
- instrument verbs
- oversized footer wordmark

Behavior:
- compressed width through variable font settings
- heavy weights for display scenes
- authored desktop and mobile line breaks
- no per-letter animation

### Instrument Sans

Role:
- body copy
- navigation
- buttons
- forms
- product names
- catalogue descriptions
- inquiry interface

### IBM Plex Mono

Role:
- product codes
- technical indexes
- metadata
- breadcrumbs
- annotations
- search ranking explanations
- catalogue document metadata

## Color ratio

Target across the homepage:
- 60–65% dark surfaces
- 17–21% green
- 8–11% blue
- 6–8% mist, steel, or bone
- no more than 4% orange, amber, coral, or violet

Green is the dominant living signal. Blue explains navigation, codes, links, and technical depth. Orange must remain rare.

## Material rules

### Steel

Steel is represented through:
- controlled silver gradients
- cool highlights
- restrained green and blue edge reflections
- no chrome text effects
- no fake photorealistic claims

### Dark surfaces

Dark areas use green-tinted near-black rather than pure black. Layer separation comes from line value, subtle gradients, and limited inset highlights rather than rounded floating cards.

### Grain

A static low-opacity technical grain may appear across dark surfaces. It must not animate, interfere with text, or resemble film damage.

### Light intermission

Bone-white is reserved for the scissors-evolution editorial chapter and other rare archive moments. It is not a default page background.

### Instrument placeholders

Placeholders must remain visibly replaceable. They may use:
- SVG line work
- silhouettes
- technical rings
- steel gradients
- generic annotation labels

They must not imply final approved photography or verified product specifications.

## Route intensity

### Theatre mode

Homepage hero, division sequence, macro examination, scissors evolution, and footer.

### Discovery mode

Products landing, division pages, family pages, and product detail.

### Utility mode

Search, inquiry, contact, legal, success, and failure states. These routes retain the identity while reducing spectacle.

## Prohibited treatments

- generic glassmorphism
- blue-only gradients
- permanent neon outlines
- random particles
- animated noise
- full-site background grids
- fake medical data
- 3D assets or 3D dependencies
- orange as a second primary color
- low-contrast technical microcopy

## Accessibility

- primary controls target at least 44px
- visible focus uses electric mint
- body and metadata colors must meet AA contrast
- reduced motion must preserve all content
- material overlays must never intercept pointer or keyboard interaction
- mobile typography must be composed rather than cropped

## Approval boundary

Sector 2 includes typography, color, and material identity only. Navigation motion, cursor inspection, scroll-linked choreography, image sequences, and Easter eggs remain excluded until later review gates.
