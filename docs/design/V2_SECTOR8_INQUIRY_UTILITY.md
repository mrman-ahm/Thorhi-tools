# THROHI V2 — Sector 8 Inquiry and Utility System

## Scope

Sector 8 redesigns the inquiry, confirmation, company, resources, contact, privacy, terms, and 404 experiences. It does not change protected catalogue, search, inquiry, API, or migration logic.

## Experience hierarchy

Utility pages are deliberately calmer than the homepage and catalogue.

- Display typography is compact.
- Motion is limited to existing microinteractions.
- Light surfaces improve form and long-form reading.
- Green indicates progress or confirmation.
- Blue indicates technical references and links.
- Orange is reserved for warnings and recovery.
- Modest radii soften the visual system without making it consumer-oriented.

## Inquiry

The inquiry is presented in four explicit stages:

1. products
2. requirements
3. buyer details
4. final review

The working logic remains unchanged:

- product and manual items
- quantities
- product notes
- removal and undo
- general requirements
- attachment metadata validation
- buyer fields
- preferred contact method
- consent
- API submission
- duplicate-submission token
- failure preservation
- success clearing

The review panel remains visible on desktop and moves into normal document flow on mobile.

## Evidence rules

Company and contact routes do not publish unverified:

- legal identity details
- public address
- phone or WhatsApp
- manufacturing capability
- certifications
- factory scale
- export reach
- OEM or private-label availability
- response times

Pending content is shown as a design and production requirement, not a public achievement.

## Resources

A resource requires approved:

- title
- division
- format
- file size
- update or publication date
- working route

Unavailable files do not render false download controls.

## Legal pages

Privacy and terms use a sticky in-page index on desktop and a normal readable index on mobile. Final legal wording, jurisdiction, effective dates, retention rules, and contact details remain launch dependencies.

## Failure and confirmation states

- API failure preserves all form and inquiry state.
- Invalid attachments explain the accepted formats.
- The success route names the development adapter honestly.
- The 404 route offers search, catalogue browsing, and manual inquiry recovery.
- No success animation, response-time promise, or retail-order language is used.

## Explicit exclusions

- final legal approval
- production database and object storage
- production email or CRM delivery
- verified direct contact details
- final company media
- Easter eggs
- final responsive audit
- production deployment
