# THROHI V2 — Sector 11 Responsive Reconstruction and Device Performance

## Purpose

Sector 11 hardens the approved cinematic, catalogue, inquiry, and hidden precision systems across real viewport classes and lower-memory devices. It introduces no new decorative effect or business claim.

## Identity correction

The previous tracked WebP rendered as an empty header and hero block in browser captures. Sector 11 installs a transparent, visible SVG identity and uses it in both the persistent header and homepage brand handoff.

The vector remains the complete THROHI Medical Tools identity. The red cross-like detail is not extracted or reused independently.

## Evolution media architecture

The 260 approved frames retain their existing chapter boundaries and source-cell quality:

- desktop cells: 640 × 360, WebP quality 90
- mobile cells: 400 × 225, WebP quality 86
- 20 frames per sheet
- 5 columns per sheet
- 13 sheets per responsive variant

The previous single-sheet design decoded approximately 243 MB on desktop and 95 MB on mobile. The bounded architecture decodes approximately 18 MB per desktop sheet and 7 MB per mobile sheet.

Runtime cache limits:

- desktop: at most 3 decoded sheets
- mobile/coarse pointer: at most 2 decoded sheets

The current sheet and one adjacent sheet are protected while older decoded images are released. The renderer still uses exactly one canvas, preserves native scrolling, and derives chapter copy from the rendered frame.

## Responsive reconstruction

The final responsive layer is loaded after all prior sector styles and establishes:

- visible header identity at every width
- compact 68 px mobile header
- touch targets of at least 44 px
- dedicated 320–340 px control sizing
- reduced-height cinematic copy and entry control placement
- reduced-height evolution stage and copy scaling
- responsive homepage identity composition
- complete reduced-motion behavior
